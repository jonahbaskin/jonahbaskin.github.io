The Montenegrin Video Game Foundation

Leveling up Montenegro's Video Game industry

Created in 2023 as a project of the Ministry of Culture and Media, the Video Game Foundation is dedicated to building up a top-tier video game industry in Montenegro by supporting indie developers, integrating video game STEM education into primary and secondary school educational curricula, and advising on national policy related to games.
